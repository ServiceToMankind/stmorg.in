🌍 Event Overview

The Research and Survey Tour 2026 is a two-day field-based initiative conducted in Adilabad District, Telangana, aimed at identifying and analyzing the challenges faced by underprivileged and rural communities. The tour focuses on Education, Healthcare, and Agriculture, collecting real-world data to design meaningful and sustainable interventions.

📅 DAY 1 – SURVEY DAY

Comprehensive surveys in Education, Healthcare, and Agriculture sectors

📘

Educational Survey

Assess primary and secondary school conditions. Evaluate access to quality education in rural and tribal regions. Identify barriers such as infrastructure gaps and teacher shortages. Interact with students, parents, teachers, and villagers. Collect data on enrollment, retention, and learning resources.

🏥

Health Survey

Assess availability and accessibility of healthcare services. Coordinate mobile health units for basic medical support. Organize health camps for general checkups and screenings. Conduct health awareness sessions. Identify prevalent health issues in the community.

🌾

Farming Survey

Analyze farming practices and farmer challenges. Assess access to seeds, fertilizers, irrigation, and training. Promote sustainable and climate-resilient farming methods. Conduct awareness workshops and demonstrations. Encourage adoption of modern agricultural techniques.

📊 REPORTS & DATA ANALYSIS

📈

Data Analysis

Analyze survey data from education, health, and agriculture. Identify key challenges and improvement opportunities. Generate actionable insights through data interpretation.

📝

Report Preparation

Prepare a detailed analytical report. Include statistical data, case studies, and findings. Propose practical and actionable solutions.

📢

Presentation & Dissemination

Present findings to local authorities and organizations. Share reports via online platforms and community channels. Collaborate with NGOs, government bodies, and donors.
